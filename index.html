About Us

At DR.VS Beverages, we believe that hydration should be both refreshing and healthy. Our range of natural beverages is designed to energize and nourish you, using only the finest ingredients. Founded in 2025, our passion for wellness drives us to create drinks using minimum artificial additives, packed with nature's goodness. Whether it's the cooling Pudina Drink, the tangy Lemon Drink, or the hydrating Coconut Water, every sip is a step towards better health. Join us in our journey towards mindful and delicious refreshment!
